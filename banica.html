Banica
Banica for breakfast, banica for lunch, banica for dinner! The banica does not have an exact place in the Bulgarian's menu, because he eats it at any time in any letiant. The sweet banica - banica with apples, banica with Turkish delight, banica with pumpkin, or in its even more popular savory versions - banica with cheese, banica with spinach, banica with leeks is as important a part of the Bulgarian national cuisine as bean soup and cabbage sarma. The recipe for this banica with cheese and ready-made crusts is one of the easiest you can come across. The original recipe for the banica is with cottage cheese, but you can always replace the cottage cheese with cheese! Quick, easy and very tasty, this banica recipe with ready-made crusts is perfect for a quick breakfast or a quick dinner! In addition, this recipe is perfect for the lucky New Year's banica .

    Products:
  • 3 eggs
  • 300g white cheese
  • 400g ground crusts
  • 400g yogurt
  • 1 tea spoon baking soda
  • 3/4 tea cup oil
  • 50g butter
  • 50g flour
    Steps:

  1. The first step in the recipe for a banica with cheese and ready-made crusts is the preparation of the filling. Stir in the eggs (previously removed from the refrigerator), yoghurt, cottage cheese / cheese, 3/4 a cup of tea oil and baking soda. You can also use cottage cheese. If you use cottage cheese, add about an equal teaspoon of salt. Mix the Products well until a homogeneous mixture is obtained.

  2. In a greased pan put 1-2 leaves of the crusts, on which put about a ladle (for soup) of the mixture. Then sprinkle with a few drops of the remaining oil. On top again put a sheet or two of the crusts and again of the mixture, and finally oil. Repeat the procedure in this sequence until the crusts and mixture are finished.

  3. Grease the last sheet of crusts with oil and very carefully cut it into pieces with a sharp knife so that you can more easily cut the banica into pieces after baking. Bake the banica in a preheated 200 degree oven for about 30 minutes until the banica turns golden.
